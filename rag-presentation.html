Slide 10: Risk Mitigation

Risk Mitigation

Proactive measures to ensure POC success

Risk: Inaccurate Responses

AI might generate incorrect or misleading information

Mitigation:

  • Mandatory source citations
  • Confidence scoring
  • Human review workflow

Risk: Data Privacy Concerns

Sensitive investment data could be exposed

Mitigation:

  • On-premise deployment option
  • End-to-end encryption
  • Role-based access controls

Risk: Low User Adoption

Analysts may resist changing workflows

Mitigation:

  • Intuitive user interface
  • Training and onboarding
  • Early user involvement

Risk: Technical Complexity

System may be difficult to maintain or scale

Mitigation:

  • Use proven frameworks
  • Comprehensive documentation
  • Modular architecture

Risk Management Approach: Continuous monitoring, user feedback loops, and iterative improvements throughout the POC phase
